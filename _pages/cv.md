## Projects

### 3D Vision System Development
*2022 - present*
* Led the development of a proprietary 3D stereo vision system, culminating in a world-class stereo matching model that achieves state-of-the-art precision.
* Currently pioneering a novel technique for selective depth processing to handle challenging environments with numerous transparent objects.

### Advanced Perception for Robotics
*2020 - 2024*
* Engineered RGB-D based 6-DoF object detection and pose estimation models for robotic manipulation in cluttered environments.
* Pioneered the use of large-scale, CAD-based synthetic data to achieve superior model generalization across diverse industrial components.

### Automation Solution Deployment
*2018 - 2024*
* Engineered and deployed robust vision-guided automation solutions across Samsung's global manufacturing sites.
* Key applications include Random Bin-Picking, Box Depalletizing, high-precision Pick and Place, automated Gas Leak Inspection, and robotic assembly for TV and smartphone connectors.

### Intelligent Robot Control & Planning
*2018 - 2020*
* Developed advanced algorithms for intelligent robot manipulation, including reinforcement learning-based controllers for complex assembly tasks and novel grasp/picking planning algorithms to enhance robot dexterity.

### Low-dose CT denoising
*2015 - 2016*
* Led a team to a 2nd place victory (out of 103) in the 2016 AAPM Grand Challenge by developing a novel deep learning method for CT denoising.
* The proposed model, WavResNet, uniquely operated in the wavelet domain to preserve critical image details while removing noise.

### Super-resolution Microscopy
*2011 - 2015*
* Designed novel optical systems from a signal processing perspective, developed their reconstruction software, and co-organized the international Localization Microscopy Challenge.
